2023 Motorcycle Trip: Georgia -> Washington

Total Miles: ~9000

Time: 7 Weeks

Motorcycle: Suzuki V-Strom 650XT Adventure

Departure: Georgia->Alabama->Mississippi->Louisiana->Texas->New Mexico->Arizona->California->Oregon->Washington

Return: Washington->Idaho->Montana->Wyoming->Colorado->New Mexico->Texas->Louisiana->Mississippi->Alabama->Georgia

Overall I am unsatisfied with this trip. I broke most of the constraints I set on myself for this trip. I did not keep good habits from this trip. I really just needed... to... slow... down... and take my time.
On a more positive note, the scenery I experienced was great. I'm more of a fan of vast empty areas, so I enjoyed the views in West Texax/New Mexico/Arizona. Same with the California/Oregon/Washington coastlines.
I met up with some friends in Albuquerque, San Franciso, and Seattle which each was a great time. It was amazing to experience these cities even if it was for a brief period of time.
The V-Strom 650 is a tank. It made the trip with no issues. I changed my oil every ~3000 miles and cleaned/relubed the chain every ~1000 miles. A chain adjustment was performed at ~6500 miles, and the air filter was changed at ~9000 miles.

I am bitter thinking about this trip and not eager to tell others about it.
Fortunately, I learned a lot from it; About myself and solo traveling.
